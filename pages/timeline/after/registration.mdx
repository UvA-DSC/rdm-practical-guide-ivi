Registration in [Pure](https://pure.uva.nl/admin/workspace.xhtml)
===

The University Library has resources on [registering research](https://uba.uva.nl/en/support/research/registering-research/registering-research.html).

The majority of research outputs at IvI are in the form of:

- Papers
- Datasets
- Software
