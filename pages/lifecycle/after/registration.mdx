Registration in [Pure](https://pure.uva.nl/admin/workspace.xhtml)
===

The University Library has resources on [registering research](https://uba.uva.nl/en/support/research/registering-research/registering-research.html).

The majority of research outputs at IvI are in the form of:

- Papers
- Datasets
- Software

Papers
---

Here are the [official instructions from the University Library](https://uba.uva.nl/en/support/research/registering-research/adding-publications.html).

From [Pure](https://pure.uva.nl/admin/workspace.xhtml), click on "+ Add content" and choose "Research output".

Datasets
---

From [Pure](https://pure.uva.nl/admin/workspace.xhtml), click on "+ Add content" and choose "Dataset".

Software
---

Follow the [instructions above](#datasets) for the registration of a dataset. (NB: Best practice for this may change in the future in favor of a dedicated "Software" category!)
